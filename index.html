Cloud infrastructure consultant

About you
We think you are opinionated about how things should be, especially when it comes to architecture. You lose sleep over security holes and wake up in a cold sweat rushing to patch them.

We do not care about how you look or how you dress or what care you drive, but if you do not encrypt your machine, you can't roll with us.

If you already know Azure
then you should be familiar with several or all of these terms and techs: ASM / ARM, P2S, S2S, NSG, VNet, ExpressRoute, storage accounts, RGs.

If you are coming from another background
then you should know things like Powershell, Bash, firewall rules, batch, VLAN, subnet, Linux, Windows Server, AD, SMTP, FTP, LDAP, IIS, backup/restore, Docker, DevOps
